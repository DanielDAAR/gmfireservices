Our Manufacturer

Ruhrpumpen
Technology

Ruhrpumpen is a global manufacturer of industrial and fire suppression pumps with over 75 years of experience in high-engineering pumping technology.

With manufacturing facilities in Germany, Canada, the USA and India, Ruhrpumpen designs and manufactures pumps that meet and exceed the most demanding standards: FM, UL, API, ANSI, ISO and NFPA.

Their fire suppression line is recognized worldwide for its reliability, superior hydraulic performance and long service life.
